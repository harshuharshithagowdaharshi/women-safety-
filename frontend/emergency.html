Quick access to emergency features and assistance when you need it most. Your safety is just a button press away.

Emergency Preparedness

Create a Safety Plan

Develop a personal safety plan that includes emergency contacts, safe locations, and evacuation routes. Share this plan with trusted friends or family members.

Emergency Contacts

Save important emergency numbers including local police, nearby hospitals, trusted contacts, and national helplines. Keep these accessible at all times.

Safety Apps

Install personal safety apps on your phone that can send your location to emergency contacts, record evidence, or contact authorities with a single tap.
