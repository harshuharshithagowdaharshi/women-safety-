Personal Safety

Learn essential safety practices and techniques to protect yourself in everyday situations.

Safety Tools

Pepper Spray

Carry pepper spray for self-defense when legal in your area. It temporarily blinds attackers, giving you time to escape to safety.

Personal Alarm

Carry a personal alarm that emits a loud sound to deter attackers and draw attention in emergency situations.

Self-awareness Information

Situational Awareness

Always be aware of your surroundings. Limit distractions like headphones and phone use when in public places.

Trust Your Instincts

If a situation feels unsafe, trust your gut feeling. Don't worry about appearing rude or overreacting.

Plan Your Route

Plan your travel route ahead of time, especially when visiting unfamiliar areas. Share your plans with someone you trust.

Self-defense Techniques

Basic Striking

Learn basic striking techniques that target vulnerable areas like eyes, nose, throat, and groin to create an opportunity to escape.

Breaking Holds

Learn techniques to break free if someone grabs your wrist, arm, clothing, or puts you in a bear hug.

Verbal De-escalation

Practice verbal techniques to de-escalate tense situations and create boundaries with potential harassers.
