Safety Tips

Stay aware of your surroundings, especially at night. Trust your instincts. Share your location with a trusted contact. Avoid isolated areas. Carry self-defense tools if needed. Keep emergency numbers handy. Lock doors securely. Use verified transport. Don't overshare online. Be confident and alert — your safety matters most.

Report Incident

If you experience or witness any suspicious activity, harassment, or threat, report it immediately. Your action can prevent harm and ensure others' safety.
